Data Deletion Request

Word Search Puzzle by Balkrishna Enterprise

Under GDPR, CCPA, and other privacy regulations, you have the right to request deletion of your personal data. This page explains how to submit a data deletion request for your Word Search Puzzle account.

📋 What Data Will Be Deleted

Upon receiving your deletion request, we will permanently delete:

✓ Account information
✓ Email address
✓ Display name
✓ Profile picture
✓ Game progress
✓ Levels completed
✓ Stars & coins earned
✓ In-app purchase history*

*Purchase transaction records may be retained by Google Play as required by law.

📝 How to Request Data Deletion

1Send an email to our support address

Use the email address associated with your Google account that you used to sign in to the game.

2Use this email template

To: balkrishnagames@gmail.com Subject: DATA DELETION REQUEST - Word Search Puzzle Body: Hello, I am requesting the deletion of all my personal data associated with my Word Search Puzzle account. Email used to sign in: [YOUR GOOGLE EMAIL HERE] I understand that this action is irreversible and all my game progress, including levels, stars, coins, and achievements will be permanently deleted. Please confirm once the deletion is complete. Thank you.

3Wait for confirmation

We will process your request and send a confirmation email within 30 days.

⚠️ Important: Data deletion is permanent and irreversible. You will lose all your game progress, coins, stars, and any in-app purchases (excluding Google Play transaction records). You can create a new account after deletion, but your previous progress cannot be recovered.

⏱️ Processing Time

  • We will acknowledge your request within 72 hours
  • Complete deletion will occur within 30 days
  • You will receive a confirmation email once deletion is complete

❓ Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Can I recover my data after deletion?
A: No. Once deleted, your data cannot be recovered. Please be certain before submitting a request.

Q: Will my in-app purchases be refunded?
A: No. In-app purchases are non-refundable. Deleting your account does not entitle you to a refund. However, if you reinstall the app with the same Google account, some purchases (like "Remove Ads") may be restored through Google Play.

Q: What if I only want to delete my cloud save but keep the app?
A: Contact us at the email below and specify that you only want cloud data deleted. You can continue using the app with local saves.
